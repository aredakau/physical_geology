Mineral identification

Mineral Diversity

  • There are over 5530 minerals known in nature, and new minerals are discovered every year.
  • Each mineral has a unique chemical composition, but many minerals appear similar in shape or form.

Challenges in Mineral Study

  • With so many minerals, studying them individually is a difficult task.
  • Some minerals have similar crystal forms and can appear nearly identical to others.
  • Advanced techniques to identify minerals: such as Electron Microprobe and X-ray Diffraction.
